SOAP 1

Title

The title is a work in progress but it will relate to 'quotebook' or 'quoting'

Birthplace of the idea

For over a year, my friends and i have been 'quoting' anything funny we say in a specific channel on our discord server as a way to remeber, look back on and revisit funny moments. So I thought, what if there was a dedicated website that does exactly what we need...
So i searched.. and searched..
And I found nothing.

Description

Users would need to sign-up by creating an account. They would then be prompted to either make or join a quotebook. They would then be able to add a new quote to a book by entering the quote, the quotee, the date(and maybe time) the quote was said. Overtime they would have a large database of quotes.

Essentials:

  • Have a database for Users
  • Have a database per quotebook
  • Interact with quotebook database by adding, editing and:
  • Fliter by quotee, between 2 dates (and maybe time), etc
  • Sort by date ascending/descending (and maybe time)
  • Group by quotees

Nice to haves:

  • Quotebook stats about the book and users
